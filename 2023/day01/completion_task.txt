Q1. What is DevOps?
==> Devops is combination of two words i.e. Development and Operations.
==> Devops team manage entire development life cycle - planning, development, integrate, testing, deploy 
    and Operations.

Q2. What is Automation, Scaling, Infrastructure?
==> Automation ==> It is the process of automating each and every task with reduce human interaction.
    Scaling ==> increase and descrease the capacity as per requiremets.
    Infrastructure ==> features and functionalities offered in single platform.

Q3. Why DevOps is Important?
==> Devops helps to manage entire development life cycle.
    Devops helps to achieve Automation and provide security.
    Objective of DevOps is faster delivery.
